SCC DF — Caustic Cracking

Required Data (Reference)

Required Data Comments Value
Susceptibility (Low, Medium, High) The susceptibility is determined by expert advice or using the procedures in this section.
NaOH concentration (%) Determine the concentration of the caustic solution being handled. Consideration for heating/flashing.
Maximum process temperature Determine the maximum process temperature. Consider local heating/mixing.
Steam out? (Yes or No) Determine whether the component has been steamed out prior to water flushing to remove residual caustic.
Time since last SCC inspection Use inspection history to determine years since the last SCC inspection.
Inspection effectiveness category The effectiveness category that has been performed on the component (A, B, C, etc).
Number of inspections The number of inspections in each effectiveness category that have been performed.
